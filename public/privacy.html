Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Memoria is a photo-based trading card game. To make that work, we collect a small amount of personal data. This page lays out exactly what we collect, where it lives, and what we will and won't do with it. If anything here is unclear, write to us at hello@memoria.tcg.

What we collect

  • Account info — your email address and a display name. If you sign in with Google, we also receive your Google profile name and avatar.
  • Photos you take — the pictures you assign to dormant cards. These live in your private Memoria collection and nowhere else.
  • Gameplay data — your card collection, decks, match history, coins, completed quests, equipped cosmetics. Same data that powers your save.
  • PVP messages — the chat emotes you send during online matches. Stored only for the duration of the match.

Where it lives

Everything is stored in Google Firebase: Authentication for your login, Firestore for your save file, and Cloud Storage for your photos. We do not run our own servers. Firebase encrypts data at rest and in transit.

Who can see your photos

Only you. Memoria has no public feed, no social timeline, no shared galleries. Other players cannot view, download, or reverse-engineer your collection — not even your PVP opponents.

What we won't do

  • We will not sell, rent, or share your photos with anyone.
  • We will not use your photos to train AI, train models, or improve any machine-learning system.
  • We will not run ads on Memoria.
  • We will not track you across other apps or websites.

Deleting your data

Delete a card and its photo is removed from Cloud Storage. Delete your account by writing to hello@memoria.tcg from the email you signed up with — we wipe your save file and any photos still in storage within 7 days.

Cookies

Memoria uses localStorage only for things like remembering whether you've seen the landing page, your audio volume, and a one-time wipe flag for legacy save data. We don't use cookies for advertising or analytics.

Children

Memoria is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, contact us and we'll close the account.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we will surface a notice in the app before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page is always current.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: hello@memoria.tcg.
